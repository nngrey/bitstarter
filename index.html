Cardea is a personal health record and wellness coach. It analyzes your health information and makes personalized recommendations to help you feel better and live longer. Cardea connects your vital statistics and medical history with devices and applications that track current personal health data and expert recommendations for how you can live better. Cardea doesn't just tell you what you've done (You walked 8000 steps walked today.) It tells you what you should do!

Cardea connects with your tracking devices and apps. It aggregates your personal health data and recommendations into a user-friendly dashboard that you can consult on your phone of computer. Cardea also helsp connect you with wellness professionals, like personanal trainers and nutritionists. You can ask them basic questions about health and wellness or hire them to analyze your health data and develop personalized plans to live better and longer.

FAQ

Can Cardea replace my doctor?
No, but Cardea can help you and your doctor work better together. Cardea will help you ask your doctor the right questions and follow up with his or her recommendations.
Do I need Cardea if I am already using an app like Runkeeper?
There are many great apps and devices for improving health. However, most of these tools just track one or two types of activity, and they generally focus on what you have done in the past, not what you should do in the future. Cardea works with other devices and apps to create a comprehensive picture of what you've done and what you should be doing to imporve your health.
